2024 State of the Map US - MapSwipe4Web Launch Party!

MapSwipe4Web launched officially at State of the Map US 2024, in Salt Lake City, Utah on Friday, June 7th at 2:15PM local time in a Birds of a Feather session. Participants took a test drive of the brand new product and had a discussion of how it could be used for humanitarian purposes. Presentation slides here.

This was a very exciting tool to project manage and I’m thrilled that it’s now out here in the world for all to use.

MapSwipe4Web lets mappers use a much bigger screen, can be used for experimental project types, and you can do it all directly from your browser.
